# HuXa Icon — Generation Prompt

Used with Google Gemini image generation. This prompt produced the current app icon.

---

**Design a premium app icon for "HuXa", a personal health and mind tracking application. The icon should be 1024x1024 pixels, suitable for macOS/iOS.**

**Color palette (MANDATORY — these are the exact app colors, use them):**
- Deep navy background: `#1a1a2e`
- Dark blue surface: `#16213e`
- Rich blue: `#0f3460`
- Coral red accent: `#e94560`
- Teal/mint success: `#4ecdc4`
- Light text: `#e0e0e0`

**Concept:** The icon should evoke the idea of a human mind being observed, tracked, and understood — a fusion of neuroscience and personal journaling. Think of it as a stylized brain or neural network that feels alive, with flowing pathways of data or thought rendered as luminous threads or particles.

**Style direction:**
- NOT a flat, simple line drawing — these look cheap. Go for depth, dimension, and richness.
- Use the deep navy `#1a1a2e` as the base background with subtle gradient into `#16213e`
- The main motif should use the coral `#e94560` as the primary accent — glowing, vibrant, almost like a living pulse or heartbeat signal woven through neural pathways
- Use the teal `#4ecdc4` as a secondary accent for highlights, small glowing nodes, or particle effects
- Add subtle depth: soft inner glow, ambient light diffusion, glass-like or frosted translucency on layered elements
- Think premium — like a high-end wellness or neuroscience app, not a cartoon
- The icon should have a rounded square shape (macOS superellipse) or work beautifully when masked to one
- Fine geometric details: maybe concentric rings, sacred geometry undertones, or data-visualization-inspired patterns woven into the brain/mind motif
- Subtle grain or noise texture on the background for tactile richness

**Do NOT:** Use text, letters, or words in the icon. No flat clipart. No single-color line art. No generic brain outline.

---

## Result

The chosen icon features:
- A frosted glass brain with layered, translucent folds
- A dark navy journal/notebook underneath the brain (binding visible)
- A coral red (`#e94560`) neural pathway flowing through the brain
- Teal (`#4ecdc4`) glowing nodes at connection points
- Faint handwritten notes/diagrams visible through the glass (journaling motif)
- Deep navy background matching the app theme
